    Flux Control Panel for Hercules is a fork of popular and versatile FluxCP, updated to work with Hercules server.

    Requirements

    • PHP 5.2
    • PDO and PDO-MYSQL extensions for PHP5 (including PHP_MYSQL support)
    • MySQL 5
    • Optional: GD2 (for guild emblems and registration CAPTCHA)
    • Optional: Tidy (for cleaner HTML output)
    • Optional: mod_rewrite support for UseCleanUrls feature

    Downloads

    Download zip/tar archive of lastest snapshot or checkout directly from Git/Subversion repository (links at the top of the page).
